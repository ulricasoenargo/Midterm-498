FAQ

Do I need to register first before using Grow Local kiosk?

Yes. You will need to register first from Grow Local's website since only users that has spot at community garden can sell their harvest at our kiosk.

How much does it cost to become a member?

It's free. We buy fruits and vegetables from you and you receive a full amount of money. Then we get our part from our sales at farmer market.

Can I put an ad on Grow Local interactive bulletin board?

Yes. You can post anything related to gardening or events nearby at Grow Local kiosk. We want to help reduce the amount of paper waste.

How long does it takes for the produce to be sold at farmer market after the gardener sold it to Grow Local?

Mostly the next day.

What if I can't be at the community garden when Grow Local's staff pick up my harvest?

We will pick up the item on the day and the time that you've choosen. If you want to reschedule the pick up time, you will have 24 hours from the time scheduled to cancel the pick up, just email us at pickup@growlocal.com

Is Grow Local available in my city?

Currently Grow Local is only available at San Francisco. We are looking forward to be at other cities. Please follow our twitter for updates.
